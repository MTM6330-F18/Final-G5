My experience with the IMD program has been very good and it is getting better and I see a lot of potential and I feel that I’m up to date with what’s out there in the industry now. I’m really happy that now we have lots of teachers and that brings in a lot of experience. What I enjoy most is teaching – I love teaching.
The way I see it is, IMD brings in a lot of areas under one umbrella. For example, if you are a graphic designer most of your focus would be on graphic design but in today’s day and age an employer looking to hire an employee is looking for someone who knows a little bit about everything but of course with some niche expertise in one specific area. For example if you are working as a graphic designer every now and then you would get questions about websites or photography and vice versa. I think IMD is very good as it combines everything and when you have all the skill sets such as graphic design, motion design, video, photography including the front end and back end web development you have a better demand. When you have expertise and experience in a lot of fields it is easier for you to become a Project Manager because you know how to manage things. Towards your graduation or end of the program you have to be able carve out your niche and say you are focusing on a particular area or that you are an expert in a particular area because you cannot be the master of everything.
